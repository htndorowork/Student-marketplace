How It Works ❓

Everything you need to know to buy and sell

For Buyers

How do I buy something?
Browse or search for what you want, tap a listing to see details, then hit "Add to Cart" or "Order Now." Once you place an order, the seller's WhatsApp number and details are shared so you can arrange payment and pickup/delivery directly.
How does payment work?
Payment happens directly between you and the seller — usually via cash, EFT, or a payment app, arranged over WhatsApp. We don't process payments on the platform.
Is it safe to meet a seller?
Check our Community Guidelines for safety tips — meet in public campus areas where possible, and trust your gut. You can report any listing or seller that seems off.
Can I save items for later?
Yes — tap the heart icon on any listing to add it to your Favorites, accessible from your profile.
What if something goes wrong with my order?
You can request a dispute/refund review from your Orders page once an order is marked complete, and our admin team will look into it.

For Sellers

How do I start selling?
Sign up and choose "Seller" as your role, then complete your seller profile — including which university and campuses you can deliver to — before posting your first listing.
Is there a cost to sell?
Selling requires an active monthly subscription. New sellers get a 30-day free trial automatically. After that, subscription payment is arranged directly with the admin team.
Can I save a listing without publishing it?
Yes — use "Save as Draft" when creating a listing, then publish it whenever you're ready from My Listings.
What happens when an item sells?
Mark it as "Sold" in My Listings — this is different from just hiding it, and keeps your store looking active and trustworthy.
How do ratings work?
After an order is completed, buyers can rate and review you, and you can rate them back. Sellers with 5+ completed sales earn a "Verified Seller" badge.

Still need help?

Use the "Report an issue" link in the footer to reach our support team directly.
